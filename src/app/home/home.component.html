About

That experience taught me how to operate seamlessly under pressure, anticipate needs, and deliver exceptional experiences; skills I now bring to software development and project design.

I have experience with full-stack development, RESTful APIs, and generative AI, gained through coursework as well as a summer internship at Tekin Software, where I worked on a variety of impactful projects from concept to deployment.
