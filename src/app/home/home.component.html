About

I’m a senior at Florida International University studying Computer Science and Software Engineering, and I hold a degree in English from FIU. My background in English has equipped me with strong communication, analytical, and problem-solving skills, allowing me to approach technology not just as code, but as a way to create clear, human-centered solutions.

Before transitioning into tech, I spent 15+ years in fine dining and upscale hospitality at some of Miami’s most iconic restaurants, including MILA, which was recently named the #1 highest‑grossing independent restaurant in the United States. That experience taught me how to operate seamlessly under pressure, anticipate needs, and deliver exceptional experiences; skills I now bring to software development and project design.

Today, I channel the combination of technical knowledge, communication expertise, and customer-focused insight into building software that makes people’s lives easier and more connected. I have experience with full-stack development, RESTful APIs, and generative AI, gained through coursework as well as a summer internship at Tekin Software, where I worked on a variety of impactful projects from concept to deployment.

Outside of tech, I’m passionate about music and tabletop games. On my days off, you’ll probably find me jamming out with close friends or at a local game store turning cards sideways.
